About NT Web Design

Hi I’m Noah, a Front-End Web Developer from Exeter, Devon. I like working with front end languages like HTML CSS, Sass, JavaScript and many more. I like to custom code websites where possible, and also have lots of experience working with templates and CMS (Content Management Systems) like WordPress, Squarespace and more. Do you like my website? I built this using HTML5, CSS, Sass (a CSS pre-processor) some jQuery and JavaScript. Most of the images are from Royalty Free, Creative Commons websites, and the other graphics I created using Photoshop & Illustrator.

At NT-Web Design we’re all about helping you boost your online presence, developing your business and personal projects and working with you to identify how a website can add value to you and your goals. We keep up to date with the latest technologies and versions and continually self-develop to stay with the times and take advantage of the ever changing and improving technologies that become available.

Over 15 Year’s experience working with small and micro business’s. A large part of my career was spent working directly with SME’s which include both Limited companies & Sole traders. I have a proven track record of working directly with clients to prioritise business goals and finances, boost efficiency and reduce waist, and ultimately trade into a far improved position with both long-term and short-term objectives.

I now largely work now with local trades, app builders, national logistic services and entrepreneurs in accelerating their visibility online and driving their business forwards. Please check out some of the testimonials I have received from many happy clients. If your business is struggling to get noticed online we can help!

As well as building your website we can advise on cookies and privacy and help you to choose the right options to secure your business.

Our websites include a free Content Delivery Network, this will store your files remotely and securely and enable your content to be served to visitors faster, for more information on CDN’s visit this link: Wiki-CDN

If you have a question about any of our service or would like to discuss a project or idea, please get in touch.
